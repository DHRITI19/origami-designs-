Interesting facts about Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleon are reptiles that are the part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing colors is an important part of communication between Chameleon.
  • Most chameleons have prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.
